**Presentation (P) - Weight: 0.05**

### 4. The Hard Problem Reformulation

Analyze the "hard problem of consciousness" through Q-score dimensions:

**Grounding Crisis (G = 0.35):**
- Explanatory gap between physical and phenomenal
- No clear empirical bridge between neurons and qualia
- Bottom-up: Low grounding in physical facts
- Top-down: Direct grounding in phenomenal experience

**Certainty Paradox (C = 0.95 phenomenal, C = 0.45 explanatory):**
- Maximal certainty about existence of consciousness (Cartesian)
- Minimal certainty about explanation of consciousness
- This split itself is informative about consciousness structure

**Structure Clarity vs. Content Opacity (S = 0.70):**
- We understand structure of information processing
- We lack account of why structure produces phenomenal content
- Suggests consciousness may involve non-structural properties

**Applicability Asymmetry (A = 0.60):**
- Consciousness research informs AI (behavior prediction)
- Consciousness research doesn't solve consciousness (explanatory gap)
- Practical progress despite theoretical impasse
